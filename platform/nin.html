Nintendo is Buying Out One on of Bandai Namco's Studios

Nintendo has recently reached an agreement to acquire Bandai Namco Sudios Singapore (BNSS). The studio will become a subsidiary named Nintendo Studios Singapore.

The deal calls for Nintendo to purchase 80% of BNSS shares on April 1st,2026, with the remainder to be acquired later once operations "stabalize".

BNSS already has a history of collaborating with Nintendo, contributing assets and development support for games including the Splatoon 3 series. According to

Nintendo, the aquisitions is meant to "stregthen the development structure" of the company. This is part od a broader strategy to expand and internalize

development recources rather than rely solely on external studios.
