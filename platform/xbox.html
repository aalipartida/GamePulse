New Dead Rising Game Has Leaks Revealing Settings and Other Features

According to a leak, a new Dead Rising entry, codenamed "Rec", is reportedly in development at Capcom. Productions is theorized to begin sometime in 2023

The game is said to bring back protagonist Frank West and will not be set in a mall, but on a massive enclosed movie-studio lot in Hollywood. The plot is said to

involve a deranged film director trapping Frank and other survivors in the studio and forcing them to a deadly trials. All while chaos ensues due to a zombie

outbreak. Fans may also get a return of classic franchise elements such as: Frank's camera for pictures, strict time-limit mechanics, and survivor-rescue system.

There is no official release date or confirmation from Capcom just yet, so it all remains a rumor for now.


Battlefield 6 players want changes to the XP features

Players are frustrated that XP boosters in game count down in real time, even in menus or waiting rooms. This means that if you activate a booster

while idle or waiting, you might burn much of it's time without earning any XP. Many feel that this design punishes casual or uneven-session players and doesn't

align well with the idea of a "booster" meant for progression. Some community members argue that it encourages continuos play, while others say its unfair

boosters are wasted most of the time.
