    About Us

    Here at GamePulse we have only one goal in mind:

    To provide our community with accurate, engaging, and easy-to-access news and release information across all platforms.


    We built our website to be reministant of our roots of gaming arcades. Where the smell of greasy pizza is nausiating, 8-bit is music playing in every direction, flashing lights and blacklights that make your head spin, and trying to keep count of your coins and tickets to get that prize you've been eyeballing.

    GamePulse is more than just a news site. We are a hub to keep gamers in the loop of this very fast paced industry where something new is always happening. Whether you are browsing upcoming games or searching the next patch update on yopur favorite game, GamePulse will be here to provide that reliable information. GamePulse is a website made by gamers for gamers, and you can trust us to keep you updated and informed.
